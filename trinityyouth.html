INTRODUCTION TO DISCORD

As we continue to “stay at home”, we are trying to help with the youth feeling isolated. Our youth need social interactions. It’s a way to let out emotions, learn new ways to communicate, and to grow in many ways. We want to provide a safe way to connect during this unique season of life.

We want to walk you through what it looks like to set up your student to connect with our Trinity Youth Online Community, using the incredible application, Discord! Follow along with the steps and pictures below.
